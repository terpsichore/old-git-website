HelixDX

HelixDX is a rhythmic bullet hell game with heavy emphasis on music. It plays simply with the arrow keys and mouse as the player flies a plane over the ocean avoiding obstacles. There was heavy emphasis on polish. The design tried subvert certain ideas around bullet hell games being oppressive by providing infinite lives and a scenic environment.

Astronauts

Astronauts is a short dialogue driven game. It is about astronauts on a small, two crew spacecraft.

A Game About Conservatory

A Game About Conservatory is a simple dialogue game. It is a technical and conceptual precedent for my game Astronauts.

Bored To Death ( Excerpt )

Bored To Death was a collaboration with the now defunct music collaboration [nullspace]. I did all visual aspects. It is an animation that uses a mix of digital and traditional media.
